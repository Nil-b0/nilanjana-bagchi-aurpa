Gaussian Processes

Gaussian Processes (GPs) are non-parametric models used for regression and probabilistic inference, providing uncertainty estimates alongside predictions. This makes them especially well suited for scientific and cosmological analyses.

I use Gaussian Process methods in cosmology, particularly for modeling observational data from Gamma Ray Bursts (GRBs), Hubble parameter measurements, and distance indicators to reconstruct underlying cosmological functions.
