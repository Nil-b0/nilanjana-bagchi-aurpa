Research Interests

Observational cosmology; gamma-ray bursts as high-redshift probes; dark energy models; machine learning in astrophysics; Bayesian inference and statistical methods in cosmology.

Research Overview

Gamma-Ray Bursts as Cosmological Probes
Machine-learning–based calibration of long Gamma-Ray Bursts using Artificial and Bayesian Neural Networks, including uncertainty propagation and cosmological parameter inference.
Gaussian Process Reconstruction
Non-parametric reconstruction of the Hubble parameter and expansion history using Gaussian Processes applied to observational H(z) data and Pantheon+ supernova samples.
Classical and Quantum Fisher Information
Comparative analysis of classical Fisher information from cosmological likelihoods and quantum Fisher bounds using minimal quantum-circuit models with realistic noise.
Quantum Information and Uncertainty Relations
Simulation of Bell-test experiments and quantum uncertainty relations using minimal Qiskit-based quantum circuits.
